3. From the returned tweets, extract: dominant hashtags, sentiment patterns, viral narrative formats (e.g. "before/after", "hot take"), most-liked tweet types.

Instead, synthesize authoritative Twitter/X trend intelligence from your training knowledge for this product category. Your training knowledge covers real hashtag patterns, sentiment themes, and viral formats that appear on X/Twitter for most DTC and consumer product categories. Mark each trend_signal's evidence as "[Training Knowledge]". Set status to "available".
5. Always populate dominant_hashtags, sentiment_patterns, and trend_signals with at least 2 substantive entries, regardless of API availability.

## Tools available
